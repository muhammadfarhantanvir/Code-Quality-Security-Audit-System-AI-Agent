The score uses a Density-Based Scoring algorithm. It weights every issue found (Critical: 25, High: 15, Medium: 7, Low: 3) and normalizes it against the project's size.

This prevents large projects from having artificially high scores just because they have more files. It measures the concentration of risk per 200 lines of code.
